About

Hola, I’m Alejandro. I built this website from scratch because I love tech, photography, and learning new things.

I'm a Mexican American photographer and a student of data science. I was raised by my marvelous mom, my abuelita, and my extended family. I started college at sixteen, graduated at twenty one, and I am preparing to begin a master’s program in data science next year. I have worked in banking, public accounting, technology and AI, and pharmaceutical manufacturing. Each job taught me something about systems and about how easily I can adapt.

I recently had spine surgery, and had a lot of time to reflect on life. I enjoy spending time with my loved ones a lot; my family, my girlfriend Juliet, and my friends (too many to name). While I am not rich in money (yet), I am comforted by the fact that I have an abundance of love around me.

I fell in love with photography about two years ago now, and I see it as a data collection tool of sorts. Cameras collect data on where light was at a given moment. We as humans then give that light, that data, meaning.

I appreciate you checking out my site, and I hope that you find the bravery to pursue your talents as well. Once all struggle is grasped, miracles are possible.
